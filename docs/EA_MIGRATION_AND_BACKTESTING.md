# GenX EA Migration and Backtesting Guide

Since you've experienced unnecessary drawdowns with the old `ExpertMAPSARSizeOptimized` EA, it is highly recommended to immediately switch to the native `EXNESS_GenX_Trader.mq5` which strictly adheres to our Python backend risk management configuration.

## Step 1: Remove Old EA
1. Open your MetaTrader 5 terminal.
2. In the "Navigator" panel (Ctrl+N), go to `Expert Advisors` -> `Advisors`.
3. Locate `ExpertMAPSARSizeOptimized`.
4. Right-click on it and select `Delete`. Ensure it is removed from all active charts.

## Step 2: Install the GenX Native EA
The native EA relies on the signals generated by your AI models instead of purely technical indicators.

1. Open your MetaTrader 5 terminal.
2. Go to `File` -> `Open Data Folder`.
3. Navigate to `MQL5` -> `Experts` -> `Advisors`.
4. Copy the `EXNESS_GenX_Trader.mq5` file (located in the `expert-advisors` folder of your project repository) into this directory.
5. In MT5, open the `MetaEditor` (F4).
6. In the MetaEditor's Navigator panel, find `EXNESS_GenX_Trader.mq5`.
7. Double-click to open it, and then click **Compile** (F7) at the top. Check the bottom terminal to ensure it compiled with "0 errors".
8. Return to MT5. In the Navigator panel, right-click on "Expert Advisors" and select "Refresh". `EXNESS_GenX_Trader` should now appear.

## Step 3: Configure EA Settings
Before dragging the EA onto a chart, configure its settings to point to your GenX backend.

1. Drag `EXNESS_GenX_Trader` onto the `XAUUSD` chart.
2. In the "Inputs" tab:
   - **UseAI**: Set to `true`.
   - **API_URL**: Enter the URL of your GenX backend (e.g., `http://localhost:8000` or your VPS IP).
   - **API_KEY**: Enter `default_ea` (or the `EA_API_KEY` defined in your backend `.env`).
   - **MaxRisk**: Set to `0.01` (1%). Note that the backend now also enforces this limit globally.
3. Click OK. The EA should initialize and connect to the backend (you'll see heartbeats in your backend logs).

## Step 4: Backtesting with Strategy Tester
Before trading live with real money or even live demo, run a backtest on historical data to verify the new risk limits.

1. Open the **Strategy Tester** in MT5 (`View` -> `Strategy Tester` or Ctrl+R).
2. **Settings Tab**:
   - **Expert**: Select `Advisors\EXNESS_GenX_Trader.ex5`.
   - **Symbol**: `XAUUSD`.
   - **Timeframe**: `M15` (or your preferred timeframe).
   - **Date**: Select `Custom period` (e.g., last 3 months).
   - **Forward**: `No`.
   - **Execution**: `Normal`.
   - **Deposit**: 10000 (USD).
   - **Optimization**: `Disabled`.
3. Click **Start** in the bottom right corner.
4. Review the results in the "Graph" and "Backtest" tabs. Ensure that drawdowns are strictly limited to the 1% per trade and 3% total risk we configured.
